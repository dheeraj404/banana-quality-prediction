About Banana Quality Prediction Project

Welcome to the Banana Quality Prediction project! This project aims to predict the quality of bananas based onvarious features such as size, weight, sweetness, softness, harvest time, ripeness, and acidity

We have developed a machine learning model using the Multilayer Perceptron (MLP) algorithm, which has shown promising accuracy in predicting banana quality.

To use the prediction model, simply input the values for the features mentioned above, and our system will provide you with a prediction of whether the banana is of good or bad quality.

Feel free to explore and use our prediction system for your banana quality assessment needs!
